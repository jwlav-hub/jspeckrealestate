Bio

Las Vegas's Most Trusted Real Estate Expert

Jeffrey S. Peck has built a reputation as one of the most trusted names in real estate through three pillars: integrity, expertise, and relentless work ethic. With deep roots in Michigan and experience across multiple markets, Jeffrey brings a proven track record to the Las Vegas Valley.

As both a licensed REALTOR® and Broker, Jeff brings a rare dual perspective to every transaction — he understands the market from both sides of the table. As a licensed Loan Broker, he can guide clients through the financing process and connect them with the most competitive mortgage options available in today's market. And as a licensed Builder, Jeff has an insider's eye for construction quality, renovation potential, and what truly drives a property's long-term value.

Jeff serves buyers, sellers, investors, and relocating families across the Greater Las Vegas Valley, including Summerlin, Henderson, Green Valley, Mountains Edge, North Las Vegas, and the urban core. His deep neighborhood knowledge — school districts, market micro-trends, upcoming developments, and community character — gives his clients a decisive edge in every transaction.

Jeff is also a Certified Negotiation Expert (CNE) — a credential that has saved his clients tens of thousands of dollars on both buying and selling sides. Whether he's negotiating repairs after an inspection or fighting for top dollar on a listing, Jeff's systematic approach to negotiation delivers measurable results.

Beyond transactions, Jeff is committed to real estate education. His weekly Tuesday evening webinars have helped hundreds of Las Vegas residents better understand the market, mortgage process, and investment strategies. He hosts regularly on YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok with 60-second videos that cut through the noise and deliver actionable real estate insights.

Jeff's philosophy is simple: "An informed client is an empowered client." Every conversation, consultation, and webinar is guided by that belief.
